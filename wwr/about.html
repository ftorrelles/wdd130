Rapids

Welcome to Our Rafting Adventures!

Dive into the ultimate whitewater experience with our expert-guided rafting tours! Whether you're a beginner or an adrenaline junkie, we offer unforgettable journeys through Class II-IV rapids in the most breathtaking natural landscapes.

Our history

It all started back in 2015 when a bunch of river-loving friends decided to turn their weekend hobby into a real business. With just two second-hand rafts and tons of enthusiasm, we began taking small groups down the river. We made plenty of mistakes at first (who knew snacks were so important?), but word spread and before we knew it - we'd become a real company!

Today we're a team of 15 river experts who still get just as excited about rapids as we did on day one. We've upgraded our gear, perfected our safety skills, and met thousands of awesome adventurers along the way. While we're more professional now (yes, we actually remember to bring first-aid kits), the fun is still the same - we just get to share it with more people!
